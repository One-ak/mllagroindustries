About Vansh Group

A legacy of quality and trust in the agro-industry.

Our Story

Vansh Group encompasses specialized entities including New Vanshika Bio Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd., Fish Gold Industries Pvt. Ltd., and MLL Agro Industries Pvt. Ltd.

Founded with a vision to empower the agricultural community, we have grown into one of Northern India's leading manufacturers of premium animal nutrition and agro inputs. Our state-of-the-art facility in Barabanki caters to a vast network of distributors and farmers across India and Nepal.

Mission

To enhance livestock productivity through innovative and accessible nutritional solutions.

Vision

To be the most trusted partner for farmers, leading the industry in quality and sustainability.

Vansh Group of Companies includes New Vanshika Bio Agro, Fish Gold Industries, and MLL Agro Industries.
